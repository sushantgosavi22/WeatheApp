Instruction how to Use Weather App


Wether app give you the detail description on your cities weather where it raining, cloud or Hot. You can aslo check for five days forecast of your city.

Step To Follow

Step 1- Open App Click on Home screen at the top-right corner of action bar click on map icon

Step 2- Clicking on Map Icon opens Map. You need to click on Map to add city.

Step 3- After that cities list display on home tab you can remove it as well by clicking delete icon in front of city name

Step 4- Click on any city item will open city tab showing weather details

Step 5- You can change setting from setting fragment such as Forecast unit, clear cities

Step 6- Last tab help to guid user how to use the app
